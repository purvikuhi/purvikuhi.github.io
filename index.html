I am a pre-doctoral fellow at IISC, India where I work under Prof. K.J. Vinoy. Previously I was part of TCS Innovation Labs, where I worked with Manoj Nambiar. My passion lies in tackling challenges related to emerging computing technologies, particularly in the realm of hardware-software cooperation and optimization.

I have been fortunate to work with an amazing set of researchers in the past. I recently graduated from Indian Institute of Information Technology, Guwahati, India where I previously worked as an undergraduate researcher under Babita Jajodia in the domain of mixed signal VLSI where I created a framework to automate optimized transistor sizing. I have previously interned at Defence Research and Development Organisation, India with Teza Bhaimidi and International Institute of Information Technology Hyderabad, India with Siddhartha Das in the domain of radar detection and quantum computing respectively. I was also part of Machine Learning Summer School as a fellow organized by Amazon India in the summer of 2022.

Along with research I have spent time working as an editor for Entrepreneurship Cell, IIITG. Parallely I have worked as a social media and marketing lead for Google Developer Students Club, IIITG and served as an editorial board member for TEDxIIITG.

For more details, hit me up on my email or hop to my blog where I curate my thoughts from time to time.
