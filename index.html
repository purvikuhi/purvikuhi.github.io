I am a researcher at TCS Innovation Labs, where I work with Manoj Nambiar and Rekha Singhal. I am interested in working on problems that focus on emerging computing technologies.

I have been fortunate to work with an amazing set of researchers in the past. I am currently an undergraduate final year student at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Guwahati, India where I previously worked as an undergraduate researcher under Babita Jajodia in the domain of mixed signal VLSI where I created a framework to automate optimized transistor sizing. I also interned at Defence Research and Development Organisation, India with Teza Bhaimidi and International Institute of Information Technology Hyderabad, India with Siddhartha Das in the domain of radar detection and quantum computing respectively. I was also part of Machine Learning Summer School as a fellow organized by Amazon India in the summer of 2022.

Along with research I have spent time working as an editor for Entrepreneurship Cell, IIITG . Parallely I have worked as a social media and marketing lead for Google Developer Students Club, IIITG and served as an editorial board member for TEDxIIITG.

For more details, check my CV or hit me up on my email.

You can also checkout my Medium which is my own little blog as I love writing about my passions, what interest me, what interests others, and sharing all of my thoughts with my readers.
